Tuk Aware

Tuk Aware is a Student Organization aimed at helping Students and other Personel under the influence of drugs and other Substances.

Since we believe that peer to peer counselling is the best from of counselling, this student based organization has made it easy for these people to get counsel and breakthrough from their addictions.

With Support from other NGOs and other medical Institutions we are able to provide all kinds of services one may need in his or her journey of rehabilitation.
